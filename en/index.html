About

Hello, we are Version Zero, a Brazilian game developer founded in August 2011. Early on, we created and produced with extreme quality the award-winning board game titled Caça aos Mitos and gaining prominence in the media.

Since then we have worked in partnership with major companies in the production of digital games, including Regionalismo, for the educational core of the Portuguese Language Museum.

Currently focused on authoring games, we are producing mobile games in parallel with our main project: stigmA.

 Regionalismo



Released




 The Regionality in Brazil is very differentiated. Due to the fact that the settlement of Brazil occurred in distinct and distant regions, the cultural trait of each region influenced the development of Portuguese throughout history. With this in mind, the educational center of the Museum of the Portuguese Language presents the Regionalism game, the first digital gaming experience of the nucleus.
